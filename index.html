About

Studio 3050 is a collective space dedicated to supporting the passion for photography. Established in 2019 by four friends, the studio’s mission is to promote studio photography, analogue photography and analogue development through experimentation and crossovers. To this end we curate a programme of event-based exhibitions and offer a vibrant space to emerging photographers to show their work, try things out and get feedback.
